🔐 Security

All data on this device is AES-256 encrypted with your PIN. Backup files are encrypted with a passphrase — a leaked file is unreadable without it. Lock the app with the 🔒 button whenever you hand the phone to anyone.

Backup & handover

Export an encrypted backup after every field day and send it to the owner/admin — he imports every executive's file to see the full picture. When an executive leaves, their last backup means zero knowledge is lost. Share the passphrase by phone call, never alongside the file.

How alerts work: each construction stage consumes specific materials. If a site's current stage needs a material you haven't yet WON (or marked lost/N-A), the site turns red and pulses on the map — an order is being placed by someone right now. Amber = the next stage's needs are coming up, or a follow-up is due within 7 days. Overdue follow-ups also turn red.
